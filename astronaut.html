Astronaut Thinking

Big Picture Thinking For Entrepreneurs

Astronauts in the space are in a position to see the big picture of life and universe from an outsider perspective because of physical vantage point and also because of Overview Effect which is a cognitive shift happens to astronauts when they look back at earth from space.Astronauts described the ‘overview effect’ as an experience that changed their perspective of the planet earth and human life in it,
