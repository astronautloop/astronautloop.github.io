Astronaut Thinking

Big Picture Thinking For Entrepreneurs

Astronauts in the space are in a position to see the big picture of life and universe from an outsider perspective because of physical vantage point and also because of Overview Effect which is a cognitive shift happens to astronauts when they look back at earth from space.Astronauts described the ‘overview effect’ as an experience that changed their perspective of the planet earth and human life in it,

This cognitive shift is caused by:

  • Firstly: Emotional and social detachment triggered by physical separation from their home and social context.
  • Secondly: The big picture visual frame of the universe shows everything at once and the place of earth in it , and also the role of human beings and social institutions.
  • Thirdly: Because of the lack of any kind of values, judgement, narratives and framesets which influences perspectives
The result is that the Astronauts are able to see things as real as it can be. The Astronaut thinking project and the blog aims at providing Cognitive Affordances for easy big picture thinking
