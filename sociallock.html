Social Lock-in Product

Products and product categories often emerge in domains were forces of social institutions, social practices and social status operates together and which persist and thrive by creating a social switching cost. This is what i call Social Lock-in Effect and such products are what i call Social lock-in product.This phenomena is very visible in Indian wedding market were practices and products emerge primarily due to the initiation by social elites.  Ex ; Wedding Car Rental, Higher caste practices being used etc . ( Experience from Awedip wedding invitation service).
